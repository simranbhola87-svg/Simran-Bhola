About My Work

I offer empowerment-based emotional healing support for individuals who want to better understand themselves and create healthier ways of relating to their emotions and relationships.

I am currently pursuing an MSc in Psychology, and my work integrates psychological insight with holistic, healing-oriented perspectives. Rather than focusing on “fixing” you, our work centers on helping you build awareness, emotional safety, and a stronger connection with your inner resources.

My Approach Is:

Trauma-aware and emotionally attuned to your specific needs.

Empowerment-focused, keeping you in the driver’s seat of your healing.
